Traveling to Hoi An ancient town is an interesting and memorable experience for many domestic and foreign friends. Hoi An Ancient Town is a famous tourist destination about 30km southwest of Da Nang city center. No noise or bustle, Hoi An has a peaceful and ancient beauty. The appeal of the more than 400-year-old city comes from its ancient architecture, moss-roofed houses, and colorful lantern streets. In addition, Hoi An is also famous for its special traditional festivals. Such as: village tutelary festival, commemoration ceremony of professional masters, celebration of religious saints,... and folk games such as drilling chants, rice pounding chants, hut cards Not only can you immerse yourself in the simple beauty of the old town, traveling to Hoi An ancient town you will also discover the interference between Eastern cultures from Japan, China and Vietnamese culture. Because Hoi An was originally the busiest and busiest trading port that existed under the Nguyen Dynasty about 200 years ago, it was the trade between Vietnam and other countries (Japan, China,...) that created a Multicultural and colorful Hoi An exists to this day...

Quang Binh is a province located in the central coastal region of our country, where natural and cultural elements of both North and South converge. People with experience traveling to Quang Binh commented that this land is blessed by nature. Quang Binh's area is not too large, but it has forests, seas, mountains, rivers, and plains. Quang Binh is located in the Northern Truong Son biodiversity area - home to a diverse and unique flora and fauna with many rare genetic resources. This place has a wide sea area, a long coastline with many beautiful beaches. Seafood resources are diverse and rich in species, including many rare seafood such as lobsters, tiger prawns, sea cucumbers Quang Binh is also known as the "cave kingdom" with more than 350 caves of different sizes. Phong Nha - Ke Bang National Park is recognized by UNESCO as a world natural heritage. Spiritual tourism is also a local strength. The people here are friendly and hospitable. The favorable weather - favorable terrain - harmonious people have helped Quang Binh become a bright spot on the tourism map of Vietnam. It is not difficult to understand that the keyword phrase Quang Binh tourism experience has up to 128 million searches on Google. 2. Ideal time to travel to Quang Binh Quang Binh's climate is located in the tropical monsoon region, divided into two distinct seasons: Dry season from April to August, rainy season from September of the previous year to March of the following year. Although the weather is a bit sunny in the dry season, most of the province is covered by sea and rivers so it is still quite cool. April and May do not rain but are not too harsh, which is the most ideal time to travel to Quang Binh. If you want to freely swim in the sea or stream, you can go in June and July.
